# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn patterns and identify key factors influencing customer retention and revenue. The dashboard provides actionable insights into customer demographics, behavior, subscription preferences, and revenue impact.

The analysis is based on a dataset containing 441K customer records and includes multiple interactive visualizations, KPI cards, and slicers for dynamic filtering.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn trends.
- Identify demographic factors affecting churn.
- Understand behavioral patterns leading to churn.
- Evaluate the revenue impact of customer attrition.
- Provide business insights to improve customer retention strategies.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Dataset Features

The dataset contains the following attributes:

- CustomerID
- Age
- Gender
- Tenure
- Usage Frequency
- Support Calls
- Payment Delay
- Subscription Type
- Contract Length
- Total Spend
- Last Interaction
- Churn

# Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of customer churn and revenue performance.

### KPIs
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Total Revenue

### Visuals
- Customer Distribution by Churn Status
- Churn by Subscription Type
- Churn by Contract Length
- Executive Insights

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Demographics

Analyzes customer characteristics and their relationship with churn.

### KPIs
- Average Age
- Male Percentage
- Female Percentage

### Visuals
- Customer Churn by Age Group
- Customer Churn by Gender

---

## 3️⃣ Behavior & Churn Drivers

Examines customer behavior patterns that contribute to churn.

### KPIs
- Average Usage Frequency
- Average Support Calls
- Average Payment Delay

### Visuals
- Churn by Support Calls
- Churn by Payment Delay
- Churn by Usage Frequency

### Key Findings
- Higher support calls are associated with increased churn.
- Customers delaying payments are more likely to churn.
- Lower usage frequency contributes to higher churn rates.

---

## 4️⃣ Revenue & Retention Impact

Evaluates the financial impact of customer churn.

### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Average Spend per Customer
- Revenue from Churned Customers

### Visuals
- Revenue by Subscription Type
- Revenue by Churn Status
- Revenue by Contract Length

### Revenue Insights
- Standard subscriptions generate the highest revenue.
- Churned customers account for a significant portion of revenue.
- Annual and quarterly contracts contribute most of the revenue.

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- Total Customers: 441K
- Churned Customers: 250K
- Churn Rate: 56.7%
- Total Revenue: 278M
- Revenue from Churned Customers: 135M

### Business Observations

- Customers with frequent support requests are more likely to churn.
- Payment delays strongly correlate with customer attrition.
- Contract length influences retention behavior.
- Customer engagement and usage frequency play a significant role in churn prediction.
- Customer churn represents a substantial revenue risk.
